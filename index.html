WHAT IS ABSFEST?

The Asheville Burlesque & Sideshow Festival is a celebration of performance art, creativity, and community. Over four incredible days and nights, experience epic stage shows, afterparties, workshops, and free community lectures and panels. We keep Asheville weird while nurturing creativity, community, and empowerment.
